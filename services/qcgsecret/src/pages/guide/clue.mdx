<Header title="Bell Rush" id="bell">
	4 player
</Header>

<Step>
By shooting the bell in the middle with a gun, orbs will begin to appear, you need to gather them as quickly as possible. Thoses orbs spawn in the same order in every game, so having a player close to the spawn is great!

The first orb spawn in front of the door, followed after on who spawn in the middle. The third spawn in front of the TV, in the closet room. After, the 4th one spawn in the attic on the beams, making you do a little some parkour. The fifth is just between the two doors leading to the Maho bedroom. The 6th one in the tunnel just under the piano room. The 7th is directly in front of the switch and the last one is just on a barrel in the basement.
